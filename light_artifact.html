AI Small Business Guru · light-first brand system

Credible, useful, human — without the midnight hacker cave.

Five light-theme identity directions with explicit contrast-safe surfaces, readable copy, reusable inline SVG marks, and realistic small-business application previews.

Design constraint applied

No dark wrapper masquerading as “premium.” Cards, details, tabs, mockups, and swatches are built around light surfaces with dark text and intentional border contrast.
